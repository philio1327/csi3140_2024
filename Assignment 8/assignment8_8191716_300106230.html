Question 19.4 - PHP Regex

The regular expression pattern to match the described string is:
$pattern = '/^A[A-Za-z0-9]{3}+[Bb]+[0-9]{2}$/';

The Regex works as follows:

  • A matches that the input must match a capital A
  • [A-Za-z0-9]{3} matches that the next three characters must be alphanumeric, i.e. capital or lowercase letter or a number
  • [Bb] matches that the character(s) following the 3 alphanumeric ones are the letter b or B (1 or multiple)
  • [0-9]{2} matches that the last 2 characters are numbers 0-9 and exactly 2 numbers
  • The $ at the beginning and end denotes the beginning and end of the pattern.
